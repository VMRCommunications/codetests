How Does it Work?

1

Each month children receive take home flyers featuring books selected from our extensive online inventory.

2

Parents log onto the Ignatius Book Club website to purchase products with their child.

3

Each month orders are shipped to your child’s classroom or group for FREE.

4

Orders earn bonus points which are redeemed for FREE books, religious products and school supplies for the child’s classroom, group or family.

Educators

Ignatius Book Club supports the Catholic School mission of academic excellence and spiritual growth by offering top-quality, award-winning books for every interest and reading level. Purchases earn generous rewards that can be exchanged for free books and other educational material for your classroom or group.
